Morphed Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

Morphometric ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Measurement Data: Body measurements you enter manually or through camera analysis. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Camera Access: We use your device's camera to capture images for body measurement analysis. These images are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any external servers.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics about app usage to improve our services.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases, transaction details are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We receive confirmation of purchases but do not store payment card details.

Camera Permission

Our app requires camera access to:

  • Capture photos for body proportion analysis
  • Enable pose detection for automatic measurements

All camera data is processed locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or share any images or camera data to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Send you updates about your account (if you opt in)
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your measurement data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.
  • Security: We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Payment Processors: Apple App Store and Google Play for subscription processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data by deleting the app
  • Portability: Export your measurement data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@morphometric.app

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

International Users

All data is stored locally on your device. No data is transferred to external servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

Email: privacy@morphometric.app
